Kanban Boards

Create, reorder and close columns of tasks to match your workflow. Just drag and drop cases between columns to indicate progress or re-order them to specify a different priority.

Provide Context Beyond Your Dev Team

The current Kanban column for a case is displayed on the case and list pages too, allowing other team members to know the status of each case.

They can also edit the case to update the column too.
