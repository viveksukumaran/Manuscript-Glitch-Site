From FYS to On Premises

Here's what you're missing out on!

New Look and Feel

Re-designed with speed and ease of use in mind. Manuscript is faster, with a simplified user experience that brings the most commonly used functionality to the fore.

In-App Notifications and Activity Feed

Keep up-to-date with what’s going on with your cases and wiki pages without ever having to leave Manuscript. The notifications page and activity feed provide all the case status updates you need, without having to switch between email and Manuscript.

Digest Emails

Stay informed but receive fewer emails. Enable periodic emails that cover all of the activity that’s happened in Manuscript, so you can stay on top of things.

Essential Info Where You Need It

See who is working on a specific case, who is involved in a group of cases, and notify users about case changes by subscribing them to case updates.

Search Guide with Auto-complete

A faster way to find the information you need. Slice and dice your Manuscript cases by building searches to find specific sets of cases.

Iteration Planner

Iteration Planner unlocks the power of Manuscript as an Agile planning tool for software development.

Graphically group cases in sprints, distribute tasks among your team with drag-and-drop and see how much work has been done toward each of your goals.

Smart Integrations

Manuscript has intelligent connections to the collaboration tools you use, like Slack, Twitter and Google Docs. Plus, they’re all powered by Glitch for ease of customization. So you can make them do exactly what you need.

Enhanced Security

Session Management, Single Sign-On with SAML, and True Delete make sure you’re in control of who has access to Manuscript and that any sensitive data isn’t accidentally left in cases.

Project Groups

Group projects so you can get the information you need in a flash and rapidly make search queries to find the exact cases you need.

And Lots More!

  • Start Work Button - for easier time tracking
  • Merge Cases - Elegantly merge duplicate cases
  • Postpone Cases - Close cases and have them reactivate when you need them
